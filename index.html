IV.

Our Story

The steppe has always known
what the body needs.

Est. 2026 · Ulaanbaatar

Long before supermarkets and seed oils, the nomads of the Mongolian steppe subsisted on the richest parts of their animals — including the marrow inside the bones. It was warmth in winter, energy on horseback, and medicine for tired joints.

Prime Pasture was born from a desire to bring that ancestral wisdom into the modern kitchen — without compromising on purity, without shortcuts, and without the industrial processing that strips modern oils of everything worthwhile.

We work directly with Mongolian herding families whose cattle roam the open steppe year-round, never confined, never grain-fed. Every bottle reflects their landscape, their animals, and their way of life.
